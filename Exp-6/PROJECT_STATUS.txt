VALIDATION RULES:
----------------
Registration Form:
  - Username: 3-20 chars, /^[a-zA-Z0-9_]+$/
  - Email: Valid email format
  - Password: Min 8, uppercase, lowercase, digit, special char
  - Confirm: Must match password
  - Terms: Must be checked

Profile Form:
  - Names: Required
  - Email: Required, valid format
  - Phone: 10 digits
  - DOB: Not in future
  - Gender: Required
  - Country: Required
  - Website: Valid URL
  - Bio: Max 500 chars
  - Skills: Min 1 required
  - Image: Max 2MB, images only

Multi-Step Form:
  - Step 1: All required, email format
  - Step 2: Phone 10 digits, Zip 6 digits
  - Step 3: All dropdowns + text required
